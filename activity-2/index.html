Roger Ebert's Most Hated

"Sometimes, Roger Ebert is exposed to bad movies.

When that happens, it is his duty -- if not necessarily his pleasure -- to report them (fairly, accurately) as he sees them. Whether they're so bad they're funny, so bad they're not funny, or so unfunny they're not funny, he must critique them.

From bad Elvis to Deuce Bigalow, these are excerpts from reviews of some of the worst movies he's ever seen. It's not just their measly ratings -- from zero to 1.5 stars -- but what Ebert has to say about them that really conveys their true awfulness."
